About Me

I am a PhD student at the Functional Imaging Lab of the German Primate Center in Göttingen, Germany. My research focuses on the effects of anesthesia on brain function using functional Magnetic Resonance Imaging (fMRI). I work with neuroimaging data from humans, non-human primates, and rodents.
